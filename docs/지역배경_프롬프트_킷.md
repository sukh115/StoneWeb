**A안 (권장): 새 지역은 첨부 없이 텍스트만.** 공통 베이스가 스타일을 잡아준다. 지역 간 톤 차이가 나면 그때만 B안.

**B안: 첨부하되 아래 문구를 프롬프트 맨 앞에.**
```
Use the attached image ONLY as a style reference — match its brushwork, lighting,
and rendering quality. Do NOT copy its composition or layout.
Do NOT include: the suspension bridge, the waterwheel, the stone pillar, or the
floating platform arrangement from the reference.
This is a COMPLETELY DIFFERENT location:
```

### 01 숲기슭 (풀 버전)
```
A hand-painted 2D side-scrolling game background in monochrome grayscale, wide 2:1 format.

SCENE: The quiet edge of an ancient forest, moments after rain has stopped. The ground is a
walkable dirt path with scattered wet pebbles and shallow gray puddles that mirror a pale
overcast sky. Two or three low, tiered platforms formed by thick exposed tree roots rise
gently on the right side — climbable, with clear flat tops for platforming. A few massive
tree trunks frame the left and right edges of the image like theater curtains.

DEPTH: Three distinct layers — (1) foreground: detailed roots, pebbles, wet leaves, one
fallen log; (2) midground: the path and root platforms, medium detail; (3) background: rows
of tree silhouettes dissolving into pale fog, almost white at the horizon.

LIGHT & MOOD: Soft diffuse light from an overcast sky, no direct sun, no dramatic shadows.
The world feels indifferent and still — not hostile, just asleep. LOW CONTRAST, airy light
grays dominate. Nothing moves.

RULES: no characters, no animals, no text, no watermark, no UI. Painterly brushwork with
clean silhouettes, like a storybook illustration.
```

### 03 암벽지대 (풀 버전)
```
A hand-painted 2D side-scrolling game background in monochrome grayscale, wide 2:1 format.

SCENE: A sheer granite cliff region inside an ancient forest. Massive stacked rock faces
rise beyond the top of the frame, with narrow stone ledges and jutting shelf-like platforms
at several heights — clearly climbable. Deep vertical cracks split the rock. Sparse tough

DEPTH: (1) foreground: sharp detailed rock edges and rubble; (2) midground: the climbable
cliff face and ledges; (3) background: further cliff walls fading into gray mist, a sliver

LIGHT & MOOD: Cold flat light from above, stone textures with rough hatching. Vertical

RULES: no characters, no text, no watermark. Painterly, clean silhouettes.
```

### 09 심근 지대 (풀 버전)
```
A hand-painted 2D side-scrolling game background in monochrome grayscale, wide 2:1 format.

SCENE: The deepest part of an ancient forest, where colossal tree roots — thick as buildings

DEPTH: (1) foreground: one massive root crossing the lower frame, pipes glinting;

LIGHT & MOOD: EXTREME CONTRAST — pure whites and pitch blacks only, almost no midtones.

### 10 중심지 (풀 버전)
```
A hand-painted 2D side-scrolling game background in monochrome grayscale, wide 2:1 format.

SCENE: The luminous heart of an ancient forest — a sacred wellspring chamber. At the center,
a blinding bright pool of pure light (the source of all life). Crouched over it: a colossal

DEPTH: radial composition — everything points to the bright core slightly right of center.

LIGHT & MOOD: MAXIMUM CONTRAST — the core is blinding white with soft bloom, everything
else is near-black silhouette. The machine is not menacing; it is diligent, tireless,
indifferent — that is what makes it unsettling. Absolute stillness.

RULES: no characters, no text, no watermark. Painterly, monumental scale.
